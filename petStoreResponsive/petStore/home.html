Paws 'n Shop

Welcome!

Welcome to our humble pet store, proudly located & serving residents in the heart of Phoenix! We have high quality products to serve every type of furry and scaly pet. You can even adopt a new member to your family!

Background

Paws 'n Shop was established in 1994, opening its' doors bright and early on April 19th. Our purpose is to provide an adoption center and line of products in order to better the lives of animals and people together.

The Staff

Each member of the Paws 'n Shop staff has been specially trained in their respective roles to provide essential service to our customers. We have the skills necessary to care for our animals and service our customers. Our staff is like a close-knit family where we respect each other and thrive together through our love of animals and improving the lives of pets in general.

Love of Animals

Animals are capable of bringing people closer and adding a lovable element to any family. We are happy to provide and grow the love through our business, as we were founded upon the love of animals in our homes.

There are a few important things to consider before bringing a pet into your life! It can be a huge step in your life which tends to alter lifestyles as there are many responsibilities involed in caring for a pet. You should consider how adopting a pet might impact your future goals such as long-distance moves, new careers, marriage, or children.

While pets do require a lot of responsbilities, there are irreplacable benefits of adding a new companion to a family! Pets can reduce stress and help us relax by providing comfort. Pet owners have lower blood pressure and lower cholesteral levels and in general, this puts them at a reduced risk for heart disease.

Paws 'n Shop is a local partial non-profit organization dedicated to the cause of rescuing animals and caring for them until they're eligiable for adoption. We have highly trained employees and skilled volunteers shaping our shelter with love and care. On the business side, we sell quality-gauranteed products from other local businesses and healthy, natural food for you pet's diet.
